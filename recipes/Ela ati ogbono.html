Ela ati ogbono

Description

This a sumptuous dish common amongst the south western people of nigeria its known for its spicy, slimy and yet mind blowing interaction with the taste buds

Ingriedents
  • 1 litre palm oil
  • okra(sized quantity)
  • ogbono
  • water
  • meat
  • kpomo
  • seasoning
  • salt
  • pepper
  • iru
  • vegetable(ugwu leaf)
  • okporoko(also called maureen's fish)
  • crayfish and smoked fish

steps
  1. Measure a reasonable quantity of water and heat until it begins to boil
  2. Add some palm oil, make sure the oil forms a thin layer of film over the surface of the boiling water
  3. slice your okra and vegetables, but add only the sliced okra first and don't stir
  4. leave mixture to boil for some time, then add your okporoko,kpomo,crayfish,smoked fish. add a reasonable quantity of salt which is dependent on the individual and the prefered seasoning. then leave to boil
  5. lastly, add the vegetable and leave to steam for a while and voila, bon apetit!!
